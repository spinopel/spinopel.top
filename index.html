Creating music in various genres

I approach the creation of electronic music as an engineering art. I pay special attention to the original sound, timbre selection of instruments, and mastering of tracks. The full cycle of music creation includes a painstaking and detailed approach using high-quality VSTi.
Musical accompaniment of video games allows to influence the atmosphere of perception and emotional state of media information consumers. The feeling of being in music is created. Creativity in this activity has a wide direction for experimentation.
Producers making experimental music often use their own or existing sound libraries. When using existing collections of audio fragments (one-shots, loops), there are licensing restrictions, so it is preferable to use your own sound database.
Collecting, listening to, and selecting samples for a library expands the perception of the diversity of sounds in nature. In the future, the accumulated impression can be transformed into extraordinary musical works during their production, regardless of the style of music. The more high-quality audio is collected, the higher the chances of finding a fragment that will harmoniously fill the space with a musical composition. It happens that a randomly selected audio fragment adds character and individuality to a track. Some bright samples in sound design can keep the listener in suspense, using all sorts of pulsations and noises, different in sound and mood. When creating a collection of audio fragments, it is convenient to divide it into categories: acoustic fragments, natural sounds (rain, birds singing, forest noise, ocean waves, and other natural phenomena), human voices, noise generators, sirens, and much more. In the creative process, this eliminates long-term searches for content, which helps to maintain inspiration. A well-structured database with many high-quality samples can be used for scientific research when training neural networks and other acoustic experiments. An audio editor with batch processing speeds up the process several times for performing repetitive tasks. It provides simultaneous processing of several audio files: normalization, conversion of bit depth and sampling frequency values, conversion to various formats, insertion of silence, panorama conversion, adding a fade-in or fade-out, removing constant amplitude shift, inversion, equalization, restoration, amplification, and more.
