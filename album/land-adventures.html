Music album - Land adventures

The music album tells about adventures on planet Earth. Meeting with terrestrial and extraterrestrial creatures. Get ready to dive into the world of unusual events!

01 - Search predators near me

A tense and gloomy atmosphere with no guarantees of a positive outcome. This music helps to tune in to long-term trials of difficulties.

02 - Dog walking

The music is suitable for dog training. Rhythmic and clear, assertive and exciting.

03 - River gorge horseback ride

Imagine what it is like to sit on a horse and gallop along a mountain gorge along a stream. Fast and rhythmic music for horseback riding in picturesque places.

04 - Chameleon walking

Swaying body movements help to relax and to look at circumstances from a different angle.

05 - Chicken coop

A rhythmic and persistent composition will help develop consistency in achieving your goal.

06 - Roll call in Tibet

A meditating composition immerses you in a state of meditating and all-encompassing internal purification. Solo Tibetan pipes set the appropriate mood and perception.

07 - Coyled cobra

A tense and serious atmosphere combined with spin-like sound effects. The track is suitable for solving accumulated problems.

08 - Tabla head

Classical Indian musical instruments motivate you to achieve success and a positive perception. The sound of the tabla takes the leading place in the composition.

09 - Walking alone

Music for walking and traveling. The constant and persistent beat inspires you to do physical exercises to strengthen your body.

10 - Dusty cowboy boots

This track has a country-style mood. The funny sound of cowboy spurs will not leave fans of westerns and dusty roads indifferent.
