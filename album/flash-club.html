Music album - Flash club

The music album is based on DJ mixes for dance floors. Cheerful, energetic, fast tracks motivate you to dance and be active. This music album consists of 14 tracks prepared for clubs, gyms, walks. Evokes the mood of a party and activity to exercise.

01 - Magic of cymbals

The sound of the xylophone and cymbals combined with a light beat allows you to relax after a hard day at work.

02 - Bounce a ball in club

Loud and proud bass motivates you to jump and skip. A track for activity.

03 - Hand plane dance

The song of the shuffling hand plane for the professional woodworker.

04 - Strong shaker

Motivating music for a gym session. Also suitable for bartenders when mixing cocktails in a club.

05 - Hi-hat alarm clock

Hi-hat sounds will help you wake up in the morning.

06 - Coastal zone

The soothing sound of coastal waves. Music of resort recreation, beach relaxation, swimming season.

07 - Hard boom

A merciless bass and continuously tempo motivates you to do heavy physical work. Jumping and skipping to a song is pure pleasure. Active exercises to music.

08 - Overhead spin

Feel the spin over your head in this club mix. The uncertainty and freezing is felt when listening.

09 - Laser strobe light

The track will rock the dance floor with slow sub-bass and retrofuturism samples.

10 - LFO bell

The low-frequency oscillator plays its role as a bell. The track is suitable for morning jogging and monotonous exercises.

11 - Stay to my beat

Fast rhythmic music created based on a clock ticking sample. The track is suitable for a waiting sequence in your video.

12 - Annoying background

Crazy tempo drop, asynchronous drums, no track arrangement. The boring and unstable pace allows you to lose focus. Listen to how it works.

13 - Tunneling

Classic club track with a popular bass line. Music for subway and train drivers.

14 - Relaxing final

Ambient track for those who have nothing to hurry.
